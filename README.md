### **UMA — Unified Master Architecture**
*Created April 2026*

An attempt to informally contribute to mathematics and physics as a non-credentialed outsider. **104/104 of python tests passed confirming accuracy of mathematics.** 

UMA's development challenges were due to having only a $20 Claude subscription for tool efficiency and token limits—it probably could have been completed in forty hours rather than three weeks. The overall goal was to unify disparate mathematics — where the right artifact might already exist and simply need a different placement. So identify what's arbitrary and reimagine. In plain terms it is a stochastic metriplectic field engine.

### **On Semantics & AI**
Semantics is meaning is feeling and to me is emotion. So what's a vibe? Artificial intelligence will hallucinate but that is not the danger in consideration of individual responsibility for inferring.

Deployed systems architecture shouldn't ever be vibe coded by a machine or administered by a machine. For theirs is just a non contextual vibe as well. A human architectural envisioning must accompany it.

Non deployable problem solving however just needs a human to vibe until true— if they aren't under disillusion or delusion. This opens the door for scientific machine language vibe coding to aid in problem space exploration.
