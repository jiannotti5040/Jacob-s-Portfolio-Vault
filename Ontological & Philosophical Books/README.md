# Ontological & Philosophical Books

**Author & sole owner: Jacob Iannotti. Proprietary — all rights reserved.**

The theoretical spine beneath the engineering. These are first-principles works
on meaning, value, and becoming — the conceptual ground from which the
certification program, the invariant engine, and Chiron later grew. They are
read as philosophy, not run as code; several ideas here (the Harmony Functional,
V-Units, identity-under-transformation) became operational mechanisms downstream.

### The corpus

- **Compendium** (`Compendium.pdf`) — the root first-principles ontological work.
  Introduces the Harmony Functional and V-Units, primitives that proved useful
  across later projects.
- **Book I — The Calculus of Truth & Consciousness**
- **Book II — The Calculus of Becoming**
- **Book III — The Calculus of Transcendence**
- **Book IV — The Calculus of Creation**
- **Book V — The Calculus of Value**

### Reading order

Begin with the *Compendium* for the primitives, then the five *Calculus* books in
order (Truth & Consciousness → Becoming → Transcendence → Creation → Value). The
arc moves from what can be known, to how things change, to what they are worth —
the same progression the applied systems certify in practice.
