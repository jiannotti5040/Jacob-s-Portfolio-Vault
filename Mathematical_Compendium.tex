\begin{abstract}
This document collects, in one place and in formal notation, the mathematics implemented or
proposed across the portfolio: the exact invariant-recovery engine (Chiron), the Semantic
Invariant Calculus (semic), Holographic Continuity Theory and its Projection Calculus (HCT),
the stochastic-metriplectic physical substrate (UMA / RSLS), the governance and decision
calculus (JDICert / SoCPM), the ambiguity calculus (Infectatrum), the derived measures used by
the bridge modules (aesthetics, value, language), the combinatorics of the Caramuel atlas
(Primus), and the variational Projection--Innovation Hierarchy (PIH). Each section is tagged
with an explicit epistemic status. The unifying object is a single operation --- recover the
minimal generator of a surface under a description-length criterion, verify it by exact
held-out prediction, and \emph{refuse} (\textsc{incompressible}) when no generator in the
hypothesis class is confirmed --- applied across integers, ciphers, code, language, dynamics,
and law.
\end{abstract}

\section{The Invariant Engine (Chiron)}
%=====================================================================
The engine treats every observable as a \emph{codified surface} $s$ (an integer sequence, a
string, a ciphertext, a graph, source code) and seeks the shortest program that regenerates it.

\subsection{Minimum Description Length}
\begin{definition}[Two-part code]
For a surface $s$ and a candidate generator (model) $M$, the description length is
\[
L(s,M) \;=\; L(M) \;+\; L(s \mid M),
\]
where $L(M)$ is the bit-cost of specifying $M$ (its class and exact rational parameters) and
$L(s\mid M)$ is the bit-cost of the residual left after $M$ regenerates $s$.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[Collapse]
Let $\mathcal{H}=\bigcup_k \mathcal{H}_k$ be the union of hypothesis classes
(Section~\ref{sec:classes}). The recovered invariant is
\[
M^\star \;=\; \argmin_{M\in\mathcal{H}} \; \big[\, L(M) + L(s\mid M)\,\big],
\]
computed in exact rational arithmetic ($M$'s parameters lie in $\Q$ via
\texttt{fractions.Fraction}). Ties are broken toward the canonically simpler class, and the
margin to the runner-up is reported.
\end{definition}
This is a tractable, exact restriction of the Kolmogorov / Solomonoff ideal (shortest
generating program, uncomputable in general) to decidable classes, scored by MDL (Rissanen).
\status{Implemented \& tested. Standard MDL/Kolmogorov lineage.}

\subsection{Held-out verification and abstention}
\begin{definition}[Held-out gate]
Split $s$ into a training prefix $s_{1:t}$ and a withheld tail $s_{t+1:n}$. $M^\star$ recovered
from $s_{1:t}$ is \emph{verified} iff it predicts the tail at exact equality,
\[
M^\star(j) = s_j \quad \text{for all } j>t \quad(\text{equality, not tolerance}).
\]
If no candidate verifies, the engine returns a classified residual and the verdict
$\textsc{incompressible}$.
\end{definition}
This zero-tolerance gate is the load-bearing property; it yields the measured zero-false-positive
behaviour (no spurious fit on a prefix survives the withheld continuation).
\status{Implemented \& tested. $\approx 5{,}000$-case fuzz with $0$ false verifications.}

\subsection{Compression ratio}
\begin{definition}
With $b_{\mathrm{raw}}(s)$ the verbatim bit-size and $L(s,M^\star)$ the two-part code length,
\[
\rho(s) \;=\; \frac{b_{\mathrm{raw}}(s)}{L(s,M^\star)} \;\ge\; 1,
\qquad
\text{(saturation } \mathcal{E}=1-\rho^{-1}\in[0,1]\text{).}
\]
$\rho\approx 1$ signals no exploitable structure; large $\rho$ signals a tiny law generating a
large surface.
\end{definition}
\status{Implemented \& tested.}

\subsection{The hypothesis classes}\label{sec:classes}
Each class is a closed-form generator fitted exactly over $\Q$.
\begin{align}
\text{Constant:}\quad & a_n = c.\\
\text{Arithmetic:}\quad & a_n = a_0 + d\,n.\\
\text{Geometric:}\quad & a_n = a_0\, r^{\,n}.\\
\text{Polynomial }(\deg k):\quad & a_n = \sum_{i=0}^{k}\binom{n}{i}\,\Delta^i a_0,
\quad \Delta^i a_0 \text{ the forward differences.}\\
\text{Linear recurrence }(\mathrm{order}\ m):\quad & a_n=\sum_{i=1}^{m} c_i\,a_{n-i},
\quad \chi(x)=x^m-\textstyle\sum_i c_i x^{m-i},\\
& a_n=\sum_j p_j(n)\,\rho_j^{\,n}\ \ (\rho_j\ \text{roots of }\chi).\notag\\
\text{Periodic }(\mathrm{period}\ p):\quad & a_n = a_{\,n \bmod p}.\\
\text{Multiplicative:}\quad & a_n = a_{n-1}\,f(n)\quad(\text{e.g. } a_n=n!).\\
\text{Alternating:}\quad & a_n = (-1)^n b_n.\\
\text{Holonomic / }P\text{-recursive }(\mathrm{order}\ r):\quad &
\sum_{i=0}^{r} p_i(n)\,a_{n+i}=0,\quad p_i\in\Q[n].
\end{align}
The holonomic class captures e.g. the Catalan numbers,
$C_{n+1}=\dfrac{2(2n+1)}{n+2}\,C_n$, and the central binomial coefficients. An
\emph{interleaved composite} of $k$ lanes assigns $a_n$ to the generator of lane $n\bmod k$.
Surfaces compressed by none of these are returned as a classified residual.
\status{Implemented \& tested. $22/22$ in-scope OEIS-core sequences recovered, $7$ correctly refused.}

\subsection{Decoding as inverse recovery}
A cipher is recovered by inverting the transform that produced it: given ciphertext $c$, find
the transform $T$ (and key) with $T^{-1}(c)$ a high-likelihood plaintext under the engine's
language model; a known plaintext/ciphertext pair recovers the key directly. Classical schemes
only (Caesar/affine/substitution/transposition/base-$n$/Morse), not modern cryptography.
\status{Implemented \& tested. $42/44$ classical instances solved ciphertext-only.}

\section{The Semantic Invariant Calculus (semic)}
%=====================================================================
The Chiron spine, lifted from integer sequences to meaning, in exact arithmetic.

\subsection{Roles, edges, skeletons}
Fix finite role vocabularies: operators $\{\textsc{transfer},\textsc{construct}\}$, objects
$\{\textsc{res},\textsc{capc}\}$, horizons $\{\textsc{bounded},\textsc{unbounded}\}$.
A \emph{skeleton edge} is a morphism $\mathrm{operator}(\mathrm{object})\to\mathrm{horizon}$.
A deterministic map $\mathrm{skel}:\text{text}\to 2^{\mathrm{Edge}}$ (a lexicon $+$ word-order
rule) sends a surface to its edge set; e.g. the ``teach a man to fish'' family gives
$\{\textsc{transfer}\!\to\!\textsc{bounded},\ \textsc{construct}\!\to\!\textsc{unbounded}\}$.
\status{Self-developed theory; the map is implemented \& tested (reproducible, no model).}

\subsection{MDL invariant, refusal, and the separable solver}
\begin{definition}
For a family $F=\{O_1,\dots,O_n\}$ of surface edge-sets, with $L(S)=|S|$ and structural
residual $\Res(O,S)=|O\symdiff S|$,
\[
I(F)=\argmin_{S\subseteq U}\Big[\alpha\,L(S)+\sum_{i}\Res(O_i,S)\Big],
\qquad \alpha\in\Q_{>0},
\]
where $U=\bigcup_i O_i$ is the (finite) edge universe.
\end{definition}
\begin{definition}[Held-out refusal]
$I(F)$ is \emph{verified} iff for every $i$ the generator recovered from $F\setminus\{O_i\}$
predicts $O_i$ with $\Res=0$; otherwise the verdict is $\textsc{incompressible}$.
\end{definition}
\begin{theorem}[The collapse is separable: exact $\argmin$ in $O(|U|\,n)$]\label{thm:sep}
The energy $E(S)=\alpha|S|+\sum_i|O_i\symdiff S|$ decomposes over edges. Writing
$c_e=\big|\{\,i:e\in O_i\,\}\big|$ for $e\in U$,
\[
E(S)=\sum_{e\in U} g_e\big([e\in S]\big),\qquad g_e(1)=\alpha+(n-c_e),\quad g_e(0)=c_e,
\]
a sum of \emph{independent} per-edge terms. Hence the exact minimizer is closed-form,
\[
S^\star=\Big\{\,e\in U:\ c_e>\tfrac{n+\alpha}{2}\,\Big\},
\]
computable in $O(|U|\,n)$ --- the identical generator that the full $O(2^{|U|})$ subset-lattice
search returns.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
$|O_i\symdiff S|=\sum_{e\in U}\big([e\in O_i]\oplus[e\in S]\big)$ and $|S|=\sum_{e}[e\in S]$, so
$E(S)=\sum_e g_e([e\in S])$ with the stated $g_e$. A sum of terms each depending on a single
independent binary choice is minimized term-by-term: include $e$ iff $g_e(1)<g_e(0)$, i.e.
$\alpha+(n-c_e)<c_e\iff c_e>(n+\alpha)/2$.
\end{proof}
The exhaustive subset-lattice search is retained as a verification \emph{oracle} (and the Gibbs and
free-energy integrals of Theorem~\ref{thm:bridge} still range over the whole lattice); the
closed-form solver agrees with it on every gate family and on $3{,}003$ random families across four
values of $\alpha$. The often-cited $O(2^N)$ cost is real but avoidable --- this objective was
never exponential.
\status{Implemented \& tested ($56/56$ gates).}

\subsection{Typed hypothesis classes and constraint discovery}
The semantic search is organized into bounded, typed classes $\mathcal H_1,\dots,\mathcal H_5$ ---
graph generators, logical rules, causal templates, organizational schemas, and semantic grammars
--- each a polynomially-bounded sub-lattice of $2^U$. Collapse ranks the best generator
\emph{within} each class and then selects \emph{across} classes, breaking ties toward the more
constrained (more falsifiable) class, so MDL operates over a \emph{defined} search space rather than
an open universe. \emph{Constraint discovery} then induces the functional predicates the recovered
generator obeys (e.g.\ $\textsc{transfer}\!\to\!\textsc{bounded}$), ranked by the fraction of the
latent operator$\,\times\,$horizon vocabulary each one removes.
\status{Hypothesis classes implemented \& tested; constraint discovery proof-of-concept.}

\subsection{The bridge theorem (deterministic $=$ $T\!\to\!0$ stochastic)}
\begin{theorem}\label{thm:bridge}
Let $E(S)=\alpha L(S)+\sum_i\Res(O_i,S)$ on the finite generator space, and let
$S^\star=\argmin_S E(S)$ be unique. The Gibbs measure
$p_T(S)=e^{-E(S)/T}/Z(T)$ satisfies $p_T(S^\star)\to 1$ as $T\to 0^+$ and $p_T\to$ uniform as
$T\to\infty$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Write $\Delta(S)=E(S)-E(S^\star)\ge 0$ with $\Delta(S)>0$ for $S\ne S^\star$. Then
$p_T(S^\star)=\big(\sum_S e^{-\Delta(S)/T}\big)^{-1}$. As $T\to0^+$ each $e^{-\Delta(S)/T}\to0$
for $S\ne S^\star$, so the sum $\to 1$ and $p_T(S^\star)\to1$. As $T\to\infty$ every
$e^{-\Delta(S)/T}\to1$, giving the uniform measure.
\end{proof}
The deterministic MDL collapse is thus the $T=0$ limit of the stochastic (energy-based) collapse:
one operator, two temperatures. With free energy $F(T)=\langle E\rangle_T-T\,H(p_T)$ one has
$F(T)\le \min_S E(S)$ with equality at $T=0$, so the MDL generator is the zero-temperature
free-energy minimum.
\status{Self-developed theory; the limit is verified numerically over the exact space.}

\subsection{Curvature of the collapse landscape}
The generator space is the subset hypercube $Q_n$ with Hamming distance $d(S,S')=|S\symdiff S'|$.
For a Markov kernel $\{\mu_S\}$ (a descent-biased lazy walk) the Ollivier--Ricci curvature on an
edge $(x,y)$ is
\[
\kappa(x,y) \;=\; 1-\frac{W_1(\mu_x,\mu_y)}{d(x,y)},
\]
with $W_1$ the $1$-Wasserstein distance, computed \emph{exactly} via integer min-cost flow over
rational measures (no floats). On the Congress graph of readings, a proverb is a clique with
$\kappa>0$ and an entendre is a barbell whose bridge edge carries genuine negative curvature:
\[
\kappa(\text{entendre bridge})=-\tfrac13,\quad
\kappa(\text{reading interior})=+\tfrac34,\quad
\kappa(\text{proverb clique})=+\tfrac23 .
\]
\status{Self-developed theory; curvatures computed exactly (rational).}

\subsection{Functoriality, performatives, action}
\textbf{Domain invariance (Class VI).} For a relabelling analogy $\varphi$ between domains,
$\mathrm{collapse}(\varphi(s))=\mathrm{collapse}(s)=P$: collapse is a natural transformation
from the surface-realization functor to the constant functor at $P$ (the naturality square
commutes). \\[2pt]
\textbf{Performatives (Class VIII).} A speech act is modelled as a state map on a world
$W=(\text{obligations},\text{roles},\text{facts},\text{authority})$ with felicity preconditions
(Austin); $\mathrm{promise},\mathrm{resign},\mathrm{declare}$ change $W$ when felicitous and
\emph{misfire} (no-op) otherwise. \\[2pt]
\textbf{Action principle.} A trajectory through generator space has discrete action
$\mathcal{S}(\gamma)=\sum_t\big[d(\gamma_t,\gamma_{t+1})+\lambda E(\gamma_t)\big]$; the
energy-monotone descent path is the action minimizer (verified by enumerating geodesics). \\[2pt]
\textbf{Twin proof.} Two lexically disjoint surfaces collapse to one generator $P$ over a
combinatorial space of $6\cdot8\cdot4\cdot5\cdot5\cdot4=19{,}200$ realizations --- the semantic
analogue of the Caramuel anchor.
\status{Self-developed theory; all four implemented \& tested.}

\section{Holographic Continuity Theory and the Projection Calculus (HCT)}
%=====================================================================
HCT formalizes identity preserved under transformation. The reference implementation is
\texttt{cert\_engine.py} ($280$ tests).

\subsection{Primitive objects}
\begin{definition}
A \emph{structure} is $S=(D,\mathcal{O}_D,\sim_D)$: descriptive content, an admissible operator
family, and a recognition equivalence. Operators $\mathcal{O}:\mathcal{S}\to\mathcal{S}$ form a
non-commutative semigroup under composition with identity $\mathbf{1}$ and partial inverses.
The \emph{orbit} is $\mathrm{Orb}_{\mathcal F}(S)=\{\mathcal O_n\circ\cdots\circ\mathcal O_1(S)\}$;
\emph{provenance} $\mathfrak p(S')$ is the operator word in the free monoid $\mathcal F^\ast$
producing $S'$. A \emph{projection} $\pi:\mathcal S\to D_i$ is many-to-one with partial inverse
(\emph{recovery}) $\rho$. $S$ is \emph{identity-bearing} if
$\rho(\pi(\mathcal O(S)))\sim \mathcal O(S)$ for all admissible $\mathcal O$.
\end{definition}
\begin{definition}[Holographic redundancy]
$S$ has redundancy with threshold $\theta\in(0,1)$ if any subset $\Sigma$ of locals with
$|\Sigma|\ge\theta|S|$ admits $\rho_\Sigma(\Sigma)\sim S$.
\end{definition}
\begin{definition}[Superposition state]
$\rho=\sum_k p_k\,|I^\star_k\rangle\langle I^\star_k|$, a density matrix over plausible invariant
orbits.
\end{definition}
\status{Self-developed theory; primitives implemented \& tested.}

\subsection{Axioms (A1--A12, condensed)}
\begin{axiomx}
\textbf{A1} a non-trivial invariant orbit exists; \textbf{A2} the orbit class is preserved under
admissible action; \textbf{A3} provenance is monotone non-decreasing,
$\mathfrak p(S')=\mathfrak p(S)\cdot\mathcal O$; \textbf{A4} operators are prior to objects;
\textbf{A5} significance is a function of relational-geometry curvature; \textbf{A6} every
sufficient local participates in generating the global; \textbf{A7} agency exists at each scale;
\textbf{A8} operators recur across scales via composition-preserving morphisms; \textbf{A9}
superposition uses an augmented state $(\rho,\mathfrak p)$ separating Markovian density from a
deterministic provenance ledger; \textbf{A10} orbits generate novelty, not only preserve it;
\textbf{A11} operator selection is stochastic, $H(\mathcal O)>0$ (generic regime); \textbf{A12}
significance is defined on the metric completion of the relational space.
\end{axiomx}
\status{Self-developed theory.}

\subsection{Theorems (T1--T8)}
\begin{theorem}[T1 existence/uniqueness up to isomorphism]
Under A1, an invariant orbit exists, and orbits under isomorphic operator algebras are isomorphic
as orbit structures (the construction $\Phi(\mathcal O_n\!\cdots\!\mathcal O_1(S_1))=
\varphi(\mathcal O_n)\!\cdots\!\varphi(\mathcal O_1)(S_2)$ is a well-defined bijection respecting
the orbit relation).
\end{theorem}
\begin{theorem}[T2 identity persistence]
If $S$ is identity-bearing and $\mathcal O$ admissible, $\mathcal O(S)$ is identity-bearing with
triple $(\pi'=\pi\circ\mathcal O^{-1},\ \rho'=\mathcal O\circ\rho,\ \sim)$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{theorem}[T3 provenance conservation, generic regime]
If $H(\mathcal O)>0$ and provenance is path-injective, no admissible transformation strictly
decreases $H(\mathfrak p)$; identity-bearing structures cannot lose provenance information.
\end{theorem}
\begin{theorem}[T4 non-commutativity]
On coupled Mystery Vectors at least one pair of epistemic operators has
$[\mathcal O_i,\mathcal O_j]=\mathcal O_i\mathcal O_j-\mathcal O_j\mathcal O_i\not\equiv 0$
(measure-then-reframe $\ne$ reframe-then-measure when ignorance and paradox are correlated).
\end{theorem}
\begin{theorem}[T5 significance as scalar curvature]
On the metric completion, any significance function is $\sigma(S)=f(\kappa(S))$ with $f$ monotone
and $\kappa$ the scalar Ollivier--Ricci curvature; the reference implementation uses
$\sigma=\tanh(\alpha\,\kappa)$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{theorem}[T6 multi-scale recurrence]
There is a faithful, composition- and identity-preserving functor
$\mathfrak S:\mathcal F_{s_1}\to\mathcal F_{s_2}$ between operator families at different scales.
\end{theorem}
\begin{theorem}[T7 holographic wholeness]
For an admissible subset family, any $\sigma$ with $|\sigma|\ge\theta|S|$ recovers $S$ to
recognition equivalence, and the underlying code rate is $\ge 1-\theta$ (Singleton bound).
\end{theorem}
\begin{theorem}[T8 Lindblad necessity]
The marginal density of a superposition state, required to stay Hermitian, positive
semidefinite, and unit-trace at all times, evolves by a GKSL master equation
\[
\frac{d\rho}{dt}=-i[H,\rho]+\sum_k\Big(L_k\rho L_k^\dagger-\tfrac12\{L_k^\dagger L_k,\rho\}\Big),
\]
not by classical mixture dynamics (which would violate complete positivity).
\end{theorem}
\status{Self-developed theory; T1--T8 have constructive (informal) proofs, encoded as tests.}

\subsection{The six epistemic operators and the action principle}
On the Mystery Vector $\xi=(\xi_i,\xi_p,\xi_t,\xi_e,\xi_s,\xi_\infty)\in\R^6$

$V=-\log p(S)$, $T=-\log q(S\mid o)$ recovers Friston variational free energy as a special case.
\status{Self-developed theory; operators \& the relaxed action implemented.}

\status{Self-developed theory.}

\section{The Physical Substrate (UMA / RSLS)}

A stochastic--metriplectic field model used as a ``crucible'': a recovered law is mapped to a

\subsection{Causal flux and coupling}
The Maxwell--Cattaneo constitutive law replaces parabolic diffusion with a hyperbolic
(finite-signal-speed) flux,
\[
\tau_J\,\partial_t J + J = -\,\mu\,\nabla \Phi,
\]
where $\tau_J$ is the relaxation time and $\Phi$ the driving potential; the RSLS instantiation
couples $J$ to a memory field $M$ through a singular barrier potential $V(M)$ and a Stage-6
self-consistency step.
\status{Self-developed theory; integrators implemented \& tested.}

\subsection{Geometry: the Menger substrate}
Admissible states are placed on a Menger sponge, whose Hausdorff dimension is
\[
\dim_H = \frac{\log 20}{\log 3}\approx 2.7268 .
\]
\status{Standard result (fractal dimension); used as substrate.}

\subsection{Sensitivity: Lyapunov and predictability}
The maximal Lyapunov exponent (Benettin renormalization) is
\[
\lambda_{\max}=\lim_{t\to\infty}\frac1t\sum_{k}\log\frac{\|\delta_k\|}{\|\delta_0\|},
\]
with a finite predictability horizon (``cone aperture'') decreasing in $\lambda_{\max}$. The
suite reports a frame-dragging contrast ($\lambda\approx+1.127$ chaotic vs.
$\lambda\approx-0.044$ stable).
\status{Standard method (Benettin); contrasts are proof-of-concept measurements.}

\subsection{Open-system decoherence}
The substrate's superposition evolves by the GKSL/Lindblad equation (as in T8), with von Neumann
entropy
\[
S(\rho)=-\tr(\rho\log\rho),
\]
and a decoherence half-life tied in the bridge to the law's compression,
$\gamma=\big(\max(0.2,\min(\rho_{\mathrm{cr}},20))\big)^{-1}$.
\status{Standard (Lindblad, von Neumann); coupling to $\rho_{\mathrm{cr}}$ is proof-of-concept.}

\subsection{Field-theoretic diagnostics}
The substrate carries a complex Hamiltonian $H[\psi]$, an MSR response field, a Noether
stress--energy tensor $T_{\mu\nu}$, and an Einstein consistency residual (deviation of the
cognitive substrate from a linearized field equation); plus Koopman transfer operators, SRB
histograms on the Lorenz system, the Kuramoto order parameter
$r e^{i\psi}=\frac1N\sum_j e^{i\theta_j}$, and distributionally-robust (Wasserstein-ball) value
bounds.
\status{Standard objects, instantiated; ``Einstein residual'' coupling is self-developed.}

\section{Governance and Decision Calculus (JDICert / SoCPM)}

\subsection{The SoCPM decision rule}
With context-sensitivity $C_x$, recommendation-autonomy $A_r$, human-impact $H_p$,
mitigating-controls $M_c$, and value-alignment $V$, all in $[0,1]$, define the gate
\[
g \;=\; C_x A_r H_p - M_c(1-V),\qquad
\text{redirect/escalate if } g>T,\ \ T=0.25 .
\]
\status{Self-developed rule; implemented \& tested.}

\subsection{Epistemic partition and Truth Horizon}
Context is partitioned into Knowns $K$, Unknowns $U$, and the unknowable void $\Omega$. The Truth
Horizon is a bounded ratio of resolved to unresolved measure,
\[
\Theta = \min\!\Big(\mathrm{cap},\ \frac{m(K)}{m(U)+\varepsilon}\Big),
\]
below a threshold of which the system must escalate.
\status{Self-developed; implemented \& tested.}

\subsection{Confidence intervals and hallucination defeat}
Belief deltas carry Hoeffding/PAC intervals: for bounded $X\in[a,b]$,
\[
\Pr\big(|\bar X-\mu|\ge t\big)\le 2\exp\!\Big(\frac{-2nt^2}{(b-a)^2}\Big).
\]
Load-bearing claims are tested by Friston-style free energy
$\mathcal F = \E_q[-\log p(o,s)] - H[q] = \text{surprise} - \text{entropy} + \mathrm{KL}(q\|p)$,
together with a coherence score and a semantic-entropy ceiling; integrity is bound by
HMAC-SHA256 attestation and a Merkle root over the certificate leaves.
\status{Standard (Hoeffding, Friston, HMAC, Merkle); assembly is implemented \& tested.}

\subsection{Jurisprudence measures}
Adversarial cross-examination accepts a rival generator as ``reasonable doubt'' when its MDL cost
is within $\varepsilon$ bits of the winner (\emph{MDL parity}); precedent distance is Euclidean
on the score vector $(C_x,A_r,H_p,M_c,V)$; per-domain jurisdiction rescales the threshold $T$ and
the score weights. Provenance is a content-addressed DAG with a \emph{minimal blame set}
(smallest upstream commit set producing a challenged artifact).
\status{Proof-of-concept; self-tested.}

\subsection{Certification and the LLM accountability wrapper}
A finding certificate composes the recovered law, a Daubert / FRE~702 reliability analysis (factors
computed from the finding), a regulatory-compliance walk of the legal corpus, and a Merkle-root
attestation with a replay seed. Crucially it states its own \emph{legal status}: it is
court-\textsc{ready} input, \emph{not} a legal determination --- admissibility is decided by a court,
and the method's lack of peer review is disclosed in the analysis itself. The same discipline wraps a
language model: given an LLM output, the wrapper audits its candor, \emph{exactly verifies the
checkable claims} (numeric sequences via collapse, arithmetic), marking each \textsc{verified} or
\textsc{refused}, runs the governance gate, and attests the result --- but it never certifies a
free-text claim as true. It certifies \emph{accountability} (what is checkable, checked; the rest
explicitly flagged unverifiable), not correctness. The model proposes; the engine disposes.
\status{Implemented \& tested; the certificate is an assurance artifact, not a legal instrument.}

\section{Ambiguity and Origin (Infectatrum)}
%=====================================================================
For a surface admitting a spectrum $\Sigma$ of valid readings with probabilities $p_i$:
\[
|\Sigma| \ \text{(cardinality)},\qquad
H(\Sigma)=-\sum_i p_i\log_2 p_i \ \text{(Shannon entropy)} .
\]
Origin attribution compares reading-distributions by Jensen--Shannon divergence
\[
\mathrm{JSD}(P,Q)=H\!\big(\tfrac{P+Q}{2}\big)-\tfrac12\big(H(P)+H(Q)\big)\in[0,1]\ \text{bits},
\]
and an adversarial score discriminates engineered multiplicity (a deliberate flood) from natural
noise. (Worked anchor: the IESVS/MARIA twin plates recover at $\mathrm{JSD}=\tfrac13$.)
\status{Standard (Shannon, JSD); implemented \& tested on the Caramuel corpus.}

\section{The Projection--Innovation Hierarchy (PIH)}
%=====================================================================
A single real action on a doubled state $(q,\hat q)$ (Martin--Siggia--Rose),
$\mathcal S_{JD}[q,\hat q]$, such that
\begin{itemize}
\item its first variation gives the deterministic dynamics;
\item its second variation gives the noise covariance;
\item its non-symmetric part gives entropy production;
\item its parameter Hessian gives the Fisher information metric;
\item its exponential generates the Onsager--Machlup path measure
$\;p[q]\propto \exp(-\mathcal S_{JD})$.
\end{itemize}
Iterating the MSR doubling yields a hierarchy of fields $(q,\eta_1,\eta_2,\dots)$, well-posed at
each finite level, with explicit limits to the Kalman filter, Schwinger--Keldysh field theory,
classical mechanics, and stochastic thermodynamics.
\status{Self-developed theory (companion document); not implemented in the engine.}

Protocol recovery is a fresh instantiation of the schema: deterministic finite-state machines are

shown but cannot predict an unseen term; the recovered law can --- the architecture's payoff is
prediction, not merely size.

\section{The unifying object}
%=====================================================================
Every section above instantiates one schema. Let a surface $s$ live in a space with a
description-length functional $L$, a hypothesis family $\mathcal H$, and a recognition
equivalence $\sim$. The portfolio's core operation is
\[
\boxed{\;
M^\star=\argmin_{M\in\mathcal H} L(s,M),\quad
\text{accept } M^\star \iff M^\star \text{ predicts held-out } s \text{ exactly},\quad
\text{else } \textsc{incompressible}.
\;}
\]
Integers and ciphers (\S1), meaning (\S2), identity under transformation (\S3), dynamical
survival (\S4), admissible action (\S5), and ambiguity/origin (\S6) are the same $\argmin$-plus-
refusal read in different spaces. The defining property is not maximal recall but the discipline
of declining to certify what cannot be exactly verified. The same gate admits a probabilistic model
safely: when a language model is used to \emph{propose} candidate structures, the exact verifier
still \emph{disposes}, so the zero-false-positive floor is preserved even with a model in the loop.
This contract is realized as a single interface (\texttt{epistemic.py}): Surface $\to$ Hypothesis
Space $\to$ Constraint Space $\to$ Verification $\to$ Certificate, with the integer engine, the
semantic calculus, the governance layer, and an energy layer as four instances of it. The energy
layer (\texttt{semic\_energy.py}) sits \emph{above} exact collapse, not in place of it: exact
collapse certifies or refuses, and only on refusal does a Gibbs measure over the generator space
return an explicitly \emph{uncertified} approximation --- exact symbolic recovery with approximate
exploration around it.
\status{The schema is the genuine, load-bearing contribution.}

\subsection{The whole spine in one file}
The code that realizes this schema across sixty-three modules is also distributed as a single file.
\texttt{chiron\_monolith.py} embeds the byte-identical source of every Chiron module and wraps them
in an import hook (\texttt{sys.meta\_path}) so that each inter-module import resolves to the embedded
copy; any module then runs from the one file (\texttt{python3 chiron\_monolith.py $\langle$module$\rangle$
\dots}). The fold is \emph{lossless}---each embedding is asserted byte-identical to its origin at
build time---and adds no logic: the gates that pass for the standalone scripts pass unchanged when
run through the fold (the core-engine battery and every selftest-bearing module verify identically).
The single-file property long held by the integer engine \texttt{chiron.py} is thereby extended to
the entire portfolio.
\status{Implemented \& tested: a lossless single-file fold of the spine, not new mathematics.}
