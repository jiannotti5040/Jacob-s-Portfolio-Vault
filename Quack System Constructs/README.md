# Quack System Constructs

**Author: Jacob Iannotti. Licensed under PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0 — free to use, modify, and share for any noncommercial purpose; commercial rights reserved (see the repository-root [LICENSE.md](../LICENSE.md)).**

Earlier exploratory work, kept and labelled honestly. These were attempts at
mathematical and ontological systems-building that did **not** meet scientific
standards — parts are flawed or over-assumptive. They are preserved deliberately:
their value was the process of learning rigorous thinking, and several conceptual
seeds here later informed the certification program and the invariant engine.
Treat them as provenance and raw material, not as validated results.

### Contents

- **Compendium's Prime Epilogue** (`Compedium's Prime Epilogue.docx`) — the
  epilogue to the Compendium; speculative extensions, some unsound.
- **The Resonant Manifold** (`The Resonant Manifold.txt`) — an attempt at a
  resonance-based manifold formalism.
- **White Paper (Updated)** (`White Paper Updated.txt`) — an early systems
  white paper.

### Status

Exploratory / not peer-validated. Use for inspiration and lineage only. Where a
construct here proved genuinely useful, it was rebuilt to standard inside the
certification program rather than carried over as-is.
