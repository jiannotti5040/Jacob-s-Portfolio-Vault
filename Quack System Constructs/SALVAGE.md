# Salvage notes — what carried forward

These constructs are kept as lineage, not validated results (see `README.md`). For
completeness, here is the honest accounting of what, if anything, was usable.

- **The Resonant Manifold** — the one genuinely transferable seed is the idea of a
  system whose admissible states are *resonant eigenmodes* of a fixed geometry rather
  than free parameters. That instinct reappears, made rigorous, in the UMA Acoustic
  Sphere (states locked to spherical-Bessel zeros, nothing tuned) — see
  `../UMA Suite/`. The manifold formalism itself did not meet standard and is not
  carried.
- **White Paper (Updated)** — early systems framing; superseded entirely by the
  certification program (JDICert) and the invariant engine (Veritas/Chiron). Kept as
  provenance only.
- **Compendium's Prime Epilogue** — speculative extensions, partly unsound; the
  *sound* primitives it gestured at (value as a unit, coherence as a functional) were
  rebuilt to standard as the operationalized V-Units and Harmony Functional in
  `../Chiron/ontological.py`.

Net: one conceptual seed (resonance-as-eigenmode) survives in UMA; two primitives
(V-Units, Harmony) survive operationalized in Chiron. The rest is lineage. Nothing
here is incorporated as-is, by design.
